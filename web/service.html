01

SKI Protection Plan

Our endowmentplan offers living benifits as well as death benifits where the sum assured is increased by 2.5% every year to accommodate inflation. Although the sum assured increased , the original premium will remain the same.

Our endowmentplan can be used as a fund , which could be used to fulfill a living need of policy holder in the future such as emergency, education and for retirement. This could be extended with the following living benifits that can be added seperately,depending on the need of the policy holder.

02

SKI Investment Plan

Our investment plan is designed to protect the policy holders while enjoying attractive returns as a living benifits. The rate of return is decided on every year. In the event of death of the policy holder basic sum assured or the customers fund balance which ever is high will be payable , the policyholder can decide thr frequency of premiur payments.

Additional living benifits attached to the plan.

03

SKI Retirements Plan

Our retirement plan is designed to enjoy the monthly pension while protecting the dependentsof the policyholders. Monthly pension will be paid for fixed period of 10, 15, or 20 years as decided by the customer. For an untimely death taking place during thepremium payment period death benifits will be paid to the niminee.

Company will pay the future premium on behalf of the policyholder till the end of the premium paying term and retirement benifits will be paid from the retirement date as selected from the nominee.
